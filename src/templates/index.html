Welcome to the Happiness Predictor

Please rate the following aspects of your life on a scale of 1 to 10, where 1 indicates the least satisfaction and 10 indicates the utmost satisfaction:

Consider your personal financial situation, such as your income level, financial security, and ability to provide for your needs and wants.

Think about the level of support you have from family and friends. How strong are your social networks and how much do they contribute to your life satisfaction?

Reflect on your personal health and well-being. Consider how your health enables you to live a life full of potential and activities that you value.

Assess how free you feel in making life choices. Are you able to pursue your goals and dreams without undue restriction?

Consider the extent of corruption within businesses and government in your environment. How does this affect your trust in these institutions?

Think about your tendency to be generous and your experiences with generosity in your community. How often do you engage in acts of generosity and kindness?

While more abstract, consider your overall contentment with life that isn't captured by the other factors. This could include aspects of your life such as satisfaction with your country's direction or personal freedoms.
